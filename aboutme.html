About Me

Styling Hair Since 2019

Colleen LaBelle, known as The Hair Engineer, operates at the Soul Collective Salon located in the Central District. As a cut & colorist - she loves working with clients to create their hair goals. She specializes in effortless natural looks through lived-in blonding/balayage services, grey blending, precision cuts, curly hair & blunt bobs. Taking new clients Mondays & Tuesdays. In addition to salon services, she specializes in providing in-home haircuts for neurodiverse children in the Seattle area - booking available by email.
